OUR SERVICES

CHECK UP

Includes general check-ups and dental X-rays to monitor your oral health and for any signs of dental problems and issues.

SURGERY

Surgical procedures for your teeth, gums, jaws or other oral structures. Includes extractions, implants, and jaw surgeries.

PROPHYLAXIS AND PERIODONTICS

These are dental procedures to thoroughly clean your teeth and gums.

RESTORATIVE

Focuses on repairing damaged teeth or replace missing ones with healthy or new teeth. Includes sealants, inlays, onlays, and veneers.

PROSTHODONTIC

Also known as dental prosthetics or prosthetic dentistry. Includes dentures and crowns.

REMOVABLE APPLICABLES

Include things like removable retainers, mouthguards, glides, and dentures.

ORTHODONTICS

Focuses on aligning your bite and straightening your teeth. It helps manage and prevent of mal-positioned teeth and jaws, as well as misaligned bite patterns.

ROOT CANAL TREATMENT

Also known as endodontic treatment. It is a procedure that involves the removal of infected soft tissue inside a tooth.

PEDIATRIC DENTISTRY

Focuses on the oral health and wellbeing of children from infancy through the teen years.

BLEACHING

A dental procedure and cosmetic surgery focusing on whitening the color of teeth.

EMERGENCY TREATMENT

Provides emergency treatment to alleviate and relieve severe and high levels of pain and limit risk of infection in your teeth and gums.

RETAINERS

Helps keep your teeth in their new, correct positions after having braces.
