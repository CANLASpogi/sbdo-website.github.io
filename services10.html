BLEACHING

  • Dental Bleaching

    • Dental Bleaching refers to a procedure that deals with teeth whitening and removes stains to help you achieve a glowing, radiant smile.

    In the bleaching process, a bleaching gel which contains hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide will be applied to your tooth enamel as a lightening agent. A light source is used to activate and heat the gel.
