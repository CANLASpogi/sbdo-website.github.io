OUR SERVICES

BLEACHING

  • Dental Bleaching

    • Dental Bleaching is a procedure that focuses on enhancing the appearance of your teeth by whitening and lightening its color. This cosmetic procedure also involves the removal of stains and discolorations .

    In the bleaching process, a liquid rubber dam or a hardening resin will be applied to your gum tissue to protect your gums from bleaching agents. A bleaching gel which contains hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide will then be applied to your tooth enamel as a lightening agent. A light source is used to activate and heat the gel.

    It aims to help you in achieving that bright and radiant smile.
