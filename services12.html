OUR SERVICES

RETAINERS

  • Retainers are orthodontic devices used to maintain the position of teeth after the completion of orthodontic treatment. They are custom-made appliances designed to hold teeth in their corrected positions and prevent them from shifting back to their original alignment.

    Retainers are used to maintain keeping your teeth straight following completion of your fixed brace therapy. They are essential components of orthodontic treatment, playing a critical role in ensuring the longevity and stability of the corrected tooth positions.

    Experience the convenience and effectiveness of our permanent or fixed invisible retainers, tailored to meet your specific dental needs. Our discreet and custom-designed retainers are s eamlessly bonded to the back surfaces of your teeth, ensuring an invisible solution to maintain the alignment achieved through orthodontic treatment. With a focus on continuous stability and low maintenance, our fixed retainers provide enhanced comfort while serving as a long-term solution for your orthodontic care.
