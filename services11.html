EMERGENCY TREATMENT

  • Tooth Sedation

    • Tooth sedation, or dental sedation, refers to the use of sedative medications to help patients relax or reduce anxiety during dental procedures. Sedation is commonly used in dentistry to create a more comfortable and stress-free experience, particularly for individuals who may have dental phobia or anxiety.

    In emergency dental situations, such as severe tooth pain, trauma, or infections, providing prompt and effective care is crucial. Sedation may be considered to manage pain, reduce anxiety, and facilitate necessary emergency treatments.
