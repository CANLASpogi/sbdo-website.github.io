OUR SERVICES

ROOT CANAL TREATMENT

  • Services

    • Root canal treatment, also known as endodontic therapy, is a dental procedure designed to treat infection or damage to the pulp (the innermost soft tissue) of a tooth. The pulp contains nerves, blood vessels, and connective tissue. When the pulp becomes infected or inflamed, a root canal is performed to remove the damaged tissue and save the tooth.

    Root canal treatment is performed to save a natural tooth that would otherwise need to be extracted due to severe infection or damage. By removing the infected pulp and thoroughly cleaning the root canals, the dentist prevents the spread of infection to surrounding tissues. The procedure alleviates the intense toothache associated with infected or inflamed pulp.

    Root canal treatment is highly successful and is a routine dental procedure aimed at saving teeth that might otherwise be lost. After the treatment, patients may experience some mild discomfort, which can usually be managed with over-the-counter pain medication.
