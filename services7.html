ORTHODONTICS

  • Simple and Complicated Extraction

    • Orthodontics is a specialized branch of dentistry that focuses on the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of dental and facial irregularities. It is focused on the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of malocclusions (improper bites) and irregularities in tooth and jaw alignment. Orthodontic treatment aims to enhance both the functional and aesthetic aspects of the oral and facial structures.

    Orthodontic treatment is not limited to a specific age group, and both children and adults can benefit from orthodontic care. The goal is to achieve a healthy, functional, and aesthetically pleasing smile.
